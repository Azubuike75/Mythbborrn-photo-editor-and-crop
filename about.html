About Exact Crop

Exact Crop is a free tool for cropping and resizing images to an exact pixel size and an exact target file size — built for situations where a form, portal, or ID system rejects your photo for being the wrong dimensions or too many kilobytes, like passport and visa photos, exam or application uploads, and profile pictures with strict size limits.

It runs entirely in your browser. There's no server, no account, and no upload step — your image is processed on your own device and never leaves it. You can export the result as JPEG, PNG, WEBP, or PDF.

Exact Crop was built and is maintained by Azubuike Christian Obiora. If you run into a bug or have a feature you'd like to see, get in touch on the contact page.
