What this is for

Exact Crop is a free browser-based tool for cropping and resizing photos to an exact pixel size while also hitting a target file size — useful for passport and visa photos, government or job application uploads with strict KB/MB limits, ID badges, LinkedIn or resume headshots, and any form that rejects files over a set size. Set your dimensions and a maximum file size, and it automatically finds the compression level that fits both. Choose Cover to fill the frame and crop any excess, or Contain to fit the whole image with padding. Export as JPEG, PNG, WEBP, or PDF.

Everything happens locally in your browser — your photo is never uploaded to a server, so nothing about the image ever leaves your device.
