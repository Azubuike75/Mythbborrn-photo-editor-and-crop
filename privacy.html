Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Your images never leave your device

Exact Crop processes every image locally in your browser using the Canvas API. Nothing you upload, crop, or export is sent to a server, stored remotely, or seen by anyone else, including the developer. If you disconnect your device from the internet after the page has loaded, the tool continues to work exactly the same, because there is no server-side component involved in processing.

What we don't collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos.
  • We do not require an account, sign-up, or login.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising trackers.

Local storage & offline support

Exact Crop is a Progressive Web App. If you install it or revisit it, your browser may cache the app's own files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, icons) on your device so it loads faster and works offline. This cached data is the app itself, not your photos, and stays on your device.

Third-party services

The PDF export feature loads a small JavaScript library (jsPDF) from a public content delivery network (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) when you first load the page. That network request only fetches the library code; your images are still never sent anywhere. If this is a concern, standard browser network tools (like the DevTools Network tab) can be used to verify no image data is transmitted.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the tool after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
